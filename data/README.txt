Date		: Valid date for data
MaxT_Records	: Record maximum temperature (period of record)
MinT_Records	: Record minimum temperature (period of record)
MaxT_Red	: Red heat impact level for maximum temperature
MaxT_Orange	: Orange heat impact level for maximum temperature
MaxT_Yellow	: Yellow heat impact level for maximum temperature
MaxT_Normal	: NCEI normal (1991-2020) maximum temperature
MinT_Red	: Red heat impact level for minimum temperature
MinT_Orange	: Orange heat impact level for minimum temperature
MinT_Yellow	: Yellow heat impact level for maximum temperature
MinT_Normal	: NCEI normal (1991-2020) minimum temperature
MaxT_Obs	: Observed maximum temperature
MinT_Obs	: Observed minimum temperature
HeatRisk	: Calculated HeatRisk based on observed maximum and minimum temperatures (negative values mean data not available)
